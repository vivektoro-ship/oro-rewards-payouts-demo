The Incentive Engine · Funnel Brain Layer 6 · ENG-1680

Attribution earns the money

The system already knows who earned every loan. This engine turns that into money — on a record that can never be quietly edited, with rules the business changes itself, and a number every field agent watches grow in real time. No more month-end hand calculation.

Every incentive is one sentence: for this kind of actor on this kind of loan, pay this rate on this basis, adjusted by these gates, released on this schedule.
Office · one drafts, another approves

The rulebook business owns

Five simple blanks: who gets paid, on what amount, how much, any extra conditions, and when. One person drafts, the system shows what it will cost, another person approves. Old rules are never edited — a new version replaces them.

The three accrual states · D11

Firm

Locked the moment the loan is given. The agent sees it instantly — nothing later in the month can change it.

Provisional

This month's running total — grows as you work. Always shows how close you are to the next level.

Final

Set when the month closes. If something is fixed later, the fix is added to next month — and always shown.
